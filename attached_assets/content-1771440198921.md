## welcome to akash tours & travels

Akash Tours & Travels is a reputable travel company that has been in operation for over 12 years. With an office based in Kolkata, the company has established itself as a trusted partner for individuals, groups and corporate clients who seek quality travel solutions.

Akash Tours & Travels has managed to offer an extensive range of services. Our services include hotel booking, tour packages (both domestic and international), air ticketing, and visa services. Additionally, we have experience in handling destination wedding arrangements for accommodation and venue booking.
